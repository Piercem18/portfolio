About

Hey there!

I'm Mary, I hold a bachelor's degree in Finance, complemented by a minor in Marketing. Currently, I'm working in Sales, but something exciting caught my attention – the rapidly evolving world of tech. I've decided to embrace this new path and make the switch to the Tech industry.

To prepare myself for this transition, I completed Google's UX design certificate course and took the initiative to become a self-taught web developer, following the Odin Project as my study path. I am also proficient in Python, and I'm continuously working on honing my skills in vanilla CSS, HTML, and Javascript. Design has always captivated me, and I find myself picking up CSS with ease, always eager to learn and grow.

Though my background lies in Finance, Sales, and Marketing, I'm open to exploring all sorts of job opportunities. However, I can see myself thriving in fields like e-commerce or digital marketing, where I can leverage my diverse skill set.

My true passion and goal for myself as a designer and developer lies in crafting beautiful websites that not only showcase great design but also prove to be effective in converting visitors into loyal customers. There's something incredibly satisfying about seeing my work come to life in such a tangible way.

I’m currently open to work! So, if you have any opportunities or projects that align with my aspirations, I'd love to chat more about them. I have linked my digital business card at the bottom of this page.

Lit Language Learner

During the Google UX Design certificate course, I created the Lit Language Learner app for social good. The objective was to assist people in learning to read in a new language.
